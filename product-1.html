Sulzer is a renowned manufacturer of pumps and related equipment, offering innovative and energy-efficient solutions for various industries. Their expertise in wastewater applications is particularly noteworthy, as they provide reliable and effective systems for managing complex wastewater challenges. We are proud to be a supplier for Sulzer pumps, delivering these cutting-edge solutions to our clients.

Key applications of Sulzer pumps in wastewater management:

  • Municipal Wastewater Treatment: Sulzer pumps are used for collecting, transporting, and treating wastewater from residential and commercial areas.
  • Industrial Wastewater: Designed to handle solids and fibrous materials, these pumps are ideal for industrial waste containing tough contaminants.
  • Flood Control: Sulzer's high-capacity pumps are effective in managing large volumes of water during floods.
  • Sewage Systems: Their pumps ensure blockage-free operation, even with high solid content in sewage.
  • Dewatering: Suitable for construction sites, mines, and other areas requiring efficient water removal.

Sulzer's wastewater pumps are known for their durability, energy efficiency, and advanced features like non-clogging impellers and premium motors

Savino Barbera specializes in manufacturing corrosion-resistant pumps and mixers, designed primarily for handling aggressive and corrosive liquids. Their products are widely used in industries requiring reliable and durable solutions for challenging environments.

Types of Savino Barbera Pumps:

  • Vertical Pumps: Ideal for industrial waters and corrosive liquids, with customizable lengths up to 3000 mm.
  • Horizontal Pumps: Designed for pumping aggressive liquids and acids, featuring anti-acid mechanical seals.
  • Magnetic Drive Pumps: Suitable for clean corrosive liquids and acids, often used in chemical and pharmaceutical sectors.
  • Diaphragm Pumps: Double diaphragm pneumatic pumps for transferring chemicals.
  • Drum & Container Pumps: Used for transferring chemicals from containers, drums, and barrels.

Applications of Savino Barbera Pumps:

  • Chemical and Petrochemical Systems: Handling aggressive chemicals and acids.
  • Wastewater Treatment: Transferring and treating wastewater in industrial and municipal settings.
  • Electroplating and Surface Treatment: Used in processes like anodizing, nickel plating, and galvanizing.
  • Water Treatment: Purification, demineralization, and acidification of water.
  • Industrial Processes: Handling corrosive fluids in industries like automotive, aerospace, and food processing.

These pumps are made from corrosion-resistant thermoplastics like PP, PVC, and PVDF, ensuring durability and efficiency.

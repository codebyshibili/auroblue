Electro Magnetic Flow Meter

The Xylem Mag Flux electromagnetic flow meter is a highly accurate device designed for measuring the flow of conductive liquids in closed, pressurized pipe systems. It operates based on Faraday's law of induction, where a magnetic field induces an electromagnetic voltage proportional to the fluid's velocity. Key features include:

  • High Precision: Accuracy up to ±0.25%, even at low flow rates.
  • No Moving Parts: Ensures minimal hydraulic influence and maintenance.
  • Versatile Applications: Suitable for wastewater, drinking water, and industrial fluids.
  • Robust Design: Made with durable materials like stainless steel and fiberglass-reinforced polyester
  • Flexible Installation: Modular design allows for various configurations and easy integration.

This flow meter is widely used for applications such as monitoring water usage, detecting leaks, and measuring wastewater flow.

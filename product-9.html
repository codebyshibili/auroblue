Valves

AVK valves are high-quality, durable solutions designed for water, wastewater, and industrial applications. Known for their reliability and advanced technology, AVK valves ensure efficient flow control and long-term performance. Offering a wide range of types, they provide excellent resistance to corrosion and wear, ensuring optimal operational efficiency.

Some of the key types include:

  1. Gate Valves
  2. Globe Valves
  3. Ball Valves
  4. Check Valves
  5. Butterfly Valves
  6. Pressure Reducing Valves
  7. Air Release Valves
  8. Foot Valves
  9. Stainers
  10. Actuated Valves

These valves are designed for water, wastewater, industrial, and other critical applications, ensuring high performance and reliability.
